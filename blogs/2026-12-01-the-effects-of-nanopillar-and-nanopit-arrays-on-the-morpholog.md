This investigation compares the effects of nanopillar and nanopit surface arrays on adipose-derived stem cell morphology and osteogenic differentiation, finding that nanopit arrays promote early osteogenic differentiation more effectively than nanopillar arrays. The results have implications for biomaterial design in bone tissue engineering and regenerative medicine applications.
