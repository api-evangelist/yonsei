This exploratory qualitative study reveals that post-discharge recovery for frail Korean older adults is an adjustment process rather than a full restoration of health, based on interviews with patients and families enrolled in a transitional care program. The findings highlight the importance of family involvement and tailored support services in facilitating successful transitions from hospital to home care.
