This research explores how microbial communities influence stem cell behavior across digestive organs, examining the role of short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs), tryptophan-derived indoles, and succinate as key microbial metabolites. The study investigates how gut microbiota signals regulate host stem cell proliferation, differentiation, and homeostasis across the gastrointestinal tract.
