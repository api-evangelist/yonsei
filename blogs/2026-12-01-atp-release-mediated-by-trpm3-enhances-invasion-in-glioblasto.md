This study demonstrates that TRPM3 channels mediate mechanical stimulation-induced ATP release from glioblastoma (GBM) cells, affecting tumor invasion and progression. The findings reveal a mechanosensory pathway in glioblastoma that could represent a new therapeutic target for this aggressive brain cancer.
